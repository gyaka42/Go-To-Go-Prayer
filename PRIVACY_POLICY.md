# Privacy Policy — Go-To-Go Prayer

Last updated: 06-03-2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Go-To-Go Prayer ("the App", "we", "us") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use the App.

## 1. Information We Collect

### A) Information you provide
The App may store settings you choose, such as:
- Prayer time calculation method/provider
- Language and theme preferences
- Notification preferences (per-prayer reminders, sound/vibration choices)
- Zikr Counter settings (counts, targets, custom labels)
- Qaza Tracker settings (counters, goals, history)

This information is stored locally on your device.

### B) Location information (optional)
If you enable location features, the App may access your location to:
- Determine prayer times for your area
- Show Qibla direction based on your position
- Find nearby mosques (Mosque Finder)

You can also use the App with manual city/location selection without granting GPS access.

We do not continuously track your location in the background.

### C) Notification information
If you enable notifications, the App schedules local notifications on your device for prayer reminders and other app features.

### D) Technical data
The App may process basic technical information required for network requests (e.g., your IP address, device network details) when contacting external services. This is standard for internet communication.

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use information only to provide and improve core app functionality, including:
- Showing prayer times and monthly overviews
- Qibla direction and compass reliability features
- Mosque Finder results and routes
- Scheduling and managing prayer notifications
- Saving your personal tools data (Zikr Counter, Qaza Tracker)
- Caching data for offline use and reliability

## 3. Storage on Your Device

Go-To-Go Prayer stores certain data locally on your device (for example via device storage) such as:
- App settings
- Cached prayer times
- Zikr Counter and Qaza Tracker data
- Mosque Finder preferences (e.g., radius, travel mode, favorites)

This data remains on your device unless you delete the App or reset data within the App (if available).

## 4. Third-Party Services

To provide core features, the App may connect to third-party services:
- Prayer time data providers (depending on the provider you select)
- Map/routing apps (e.g., Apple Maps) when you choose "Open in Maps"
- OpenStreetMap/Overpass API for Mosque Finder (nearby mosques)

When the App requests data from third-party services, those services may receive standard request data such as your IP address and, where relevant, approximate location derived from your request. Their use of data is governed by their own privacy policies.

## 5. Purchases

Go-To-Go Prayer may be offered as a paid app in the App Store in the future. If you purchase the App, the payment is processed by Apple (App Store). We do not collect, receive, or store payment details (such as credit card information). We may store basic information on your device that helps the App operate correctly after purchase (for example, that the App is installed and available to you through the App Store).

## 6. Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal information.

We do not share your personal information with advertisers.

We only share information when necessary to provide a feature you use (for example, contacting an external prayer times provider or Overpass API for Mosque Finder results).

## 7. Your Choices and Controls

You can control your privacy in these ways:
- Use manual city selection instead of GPS
- Turn location access on/off in your device settings
- Turn notifications on/off in your device settings
- Delete the App to remove locally stored data

## 8. Data Retention

We do not maintain a user account system, and we do not store your personal data on our own servers for typical app usage. Data you set (settings, counters, cached times) is stored locally on your device until you delete it.

## 9. Security

We take reasonable steps to protect information used by the App. However, no method of storage or transmission is 100% secure. We recommend keeping your device up to date and protected with a passcode.

## 10. Children's Privacy

Go-To-Go Prayer is not directed specifically to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## 11. International Users

If you use the App outside your country of residence, your information may be processed on your device and, when using online features, transmitted to third-party services in other countries.

## 12. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will update the "Last updated" date above when changes are made.
